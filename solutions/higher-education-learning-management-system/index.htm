Higher education learning management system

Higher education institutions need top-notch learning technology to remain competitive and attractive to modern students, whose lifestyle is rapidly transitioning to online and mobile learning, work and communication. Learning management system helps cater those needs and provides a tremendous possibility to deliver their value to hundreds of thousands of students worldwide.

Who will benefit

  • Geographically diversified universities
  • Distance education centers
  • Career colleges
  • Corporate universities
  • Postgraduate learning centers
  • Any higher ed institutions operating globally

What are the benefits

  • 1 Much lower deployment and maintenance costs, as compared to deploying a bundle of standalone solutions.
  • 2 A variety of higher education models supported - from blended learning to fully online courses, which help cover the needs of any higher education institutions.
  • 3 Convenient tools for transition of existing content online and online course creation, which help utilize the existing knowledge with maximum efficiency.
  • 4 Optimized expenses provide an important competitive edge, allowing to better distribute the limited resources while gaining better educational outcomes.
  • 5 Security of access to the content and exams, ensuring online courses can be a viable part of the curriculum.
  • 6 Smooth integration into existing software ecosystem and interaction with various third party software due to powerful API and a variety of connectors.
  • 7 Tools for empowering student communication and collaboration, making higher education much more engaging and efficient.
  • 8 Better knowledge comprehension and retention, as well as student satisfaction with learning, by delivering multiple engagement gamification tools.

How we help

  • Learning management system deployment, setup and maintenance
  • Custom feature development, integration and configuration
  • Assistance and consulting with content design and development
  • Ensuring security using Single Sign-on feature and OAuth2 authentication system
  • Integrating the learning management system into an existing software ecosystem
  • Configuring various collaboration and communication tools along with gamification, social networks integration, etc.
  • Delivering strict hierarchy of access levels (course author, staff members, exam proctors, students) to provide sophisticated approach to learning outcomes monitoring and control.
  • Providing tools for certification, allowing counting the online courses as a part of the curriculum.
