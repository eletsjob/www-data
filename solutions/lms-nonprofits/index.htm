LMS for nonprofits

Nonprofits pursue noble goals but need global reach and substantial support to deliver a notable impact. This always means some exchange of the information. Learning management system is a great solution allowing to exchange information and knowledge efficiently, especially for a geographically diversified volunteer pool, peers, investors, etc.

Who will benefit

  • Directors and managers
  • Volunteers and employees
  • Donors and community members

What are the benefits

  • 1 Low deployment, setup and maintenance fees as it is based on an open-source top-notch online learning platform
  • 2 Wide branding, theming and customization options to deliver consistent nonprofit’s image
  • 3 Easy integration of this online training solution with other software important for the nonprofit, due to powerful API and connectors
  • 4 Easy training content creation and management helping to minimize the learning materials production spans due to potent LCMS
  • 5 Convenient learner management to follow any learner’s status updates quickly
  • 6 Empowerment of communication and collaboration via push notifications, social network integration, discussion forums, and separate team channels (cohorts)
  • 7 Convenient training efficiency measurement of nonprofit’s volunteers and employees via detailed analytics and statistics system
  • 8 Deeper learner’s immersion into training through various engagement and gamification tools like leaderboards, appraisals, badges for valuable achievements, etc.
  • 9 A simple and efficient way to keep the global community engaged with the nonprofit’s efforts, achievements and important milestones via a one-for-all eLearning solution

How we help

  • We help deliver efficient online training for employees and volunteers in short terms and within minimum budget
  • We brand and customize the solution to align with the nonprofit’s vision, motto and website appearance
  • We help create the online training materials and provide consulting on delivering the most impact with them through the use of instructional design and multiple engagement features
  • We integrate various communication channels like third party modules and social networks to ensure the best possible communication and collaboration is available to your community
  • We help set different access levels for course authors and learners, as well as providing personal course access to individuals
  • We configure the analytics precisely to help measure the efficiency of training strategies chosen and adjust them if need be
