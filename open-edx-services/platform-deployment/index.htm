Open edX Hosting

We have hosted and configured over 100 Open edX-based online learning platforms on Windows, Ubuntu, Centos, etc. This experience allows us to ensure stability and security of every instance of Open edX platform we host.

Open edX hosting and configuration is a rather complicated process. Detailed guides on it are available, yet require knowledge and experience with software products like Vagrant, Ansible, Docker and VirtualBox, along with ample understanding of Ubuntu features. As correct Open edX platform installation is crucial for the stability and efficiency of online courses or corporate training using Open edX, hiring professionals for this task is highly recommended, especially if you want to setup Open edX on Windows or Centos.
